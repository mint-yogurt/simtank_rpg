area blobs: 
<lawn> - grass tiles in front of every <house> tile, as well as randomly filling the town maps
<gravel> - gravel tiles - used for driveways and streets and occasional random blobs. 
<cobble> - cobble stone floor - random cobble floor blobs in towns, 
<dirt> spawns within large grass blobs. either self contained with corners, or connects to another dirt blob via dirt paths.
not all towns need all of these blobs, bigger towns will have most of them. both houseA and B equally likely

all the area ground blobs are spawned before any buildings are spawned. buildings are placed on top of ground tiles

stone buildings: sometimes rectangular, square, even pyramidal. ocassionally has short 2-3 tile towers made of stoneWindow tiles. sometimes has roof1, other times no roof and just the stone building topper tiles

house A: can be as small as 2x2 (using exclusive roof tiles for this size) and other varying sizes
house B: identical to houseA, but using variant tiles.

healer huts: one in each town generation. MUST have walkable tiles in front of it so players can walk directly up to it. not random size, always 3 wide by 2 tall using each of the tiles labeled for healer hut

these 2 never change: black 000000, white fcfcfc
